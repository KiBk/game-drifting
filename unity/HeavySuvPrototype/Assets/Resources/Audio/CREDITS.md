# Vehicle Audio Credits

The prototype loads these clips from `Resources/Audio` and layers them at runtime.

- `ev_motor_low.mp3` is adapted from **Generator (loop)** by YCbCr, licensed CC0.
  Source: https://opengameart.org/content/generator-loop
- `ev_motor_high.wav` is adapted from **(defect) motor loop** by qubodup, licensed CC0.
  Source: https://opengameart.org/content/defect-motor-loop
- `tire_rolling.ogg` comes from **30 CC0 SFX loops** by rubberduck, licensed CC0.
  Source: https://opengameart.org/content/30-cc0-sfx-loops
- `tire_squeal.wav` is adapted from **Car tire squeal skid loop** by audible-edge / Tom Haigh,
  licensed CC BY 3.0.
  Source: https://lpc.opengameart.org/content/car-tire-squeal-skid-loop
  License: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/

The motor-high and tire-squeal files were converted to mono 44.1 kHz PCM for this Unity prototype.
